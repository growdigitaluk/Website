This Cookie Policy explains how Grow Digital UK ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for basic website functionality Session
Analytics Help us understand how visitors use our site Up to 2 years
Functional Remember your preferences Up to 1 year

Third-Party Cookies

Our website may include content from third parties that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Social Media: If you share content to social platforms
  • Formspree: For contact form functionality

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Allow cookies from specific sites only

Note: Blocking certain cookies may affect website functionality.

How to Disable Cookies

To disable cookies in common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions
